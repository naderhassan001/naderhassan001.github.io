About

Egypt born and Boston raised! I grow plants in the backyard you could never imagine could be grown in Boston. My garden has everything from tomatoes to peaches to watermelons to parsley and garlic. Slightly north of the city is where I reside. I am grateful for the seasons and the rich history of my state! My background is in biochemistry with professional licenses in pharmacology and compounding. The past year and a half have been filled with QA and project management experience in a pharmacy setting. Recently, I graduated from MIT's Full Stack Software Engineering program. My degree is the first step into diving into software development. As a Boston resident I see my community hurt by the lack of access to basic resources. Boston still has communities that are food deserts and communities that lack access to basic city resources. Boston does not suffer from a lack of resources; it suffers from a lack of access to those resources. Tech can help all these issues and bring people and families that are marginalized into the fold of public resources. That's my mission and goal for my community.
